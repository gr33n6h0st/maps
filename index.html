LENS 01 // SPATIAL ANTAGONISM

The Blind Curve

Hanover Public School suffers from geometric antagonism. The street layout features a sharp curve that actively hides small pedestrians. At 40 km/h, the sightline deficit creates a 'Visibility Trap'.

LOG: THE HANOVER GEOMETRY
Driver sightlines are reduced by 40% due to curve radius. Drivers have less than 2 seconds to react to a child emerging from behind snow windrows.
