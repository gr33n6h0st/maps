The Blind Curve

Hanover Public School's approach features a sharp, blind curve. Street geometry obscures children from driver view, creating a mechanical impossibility for safe stopping at current speed limits.

LOG: FEB 12
Drivers entering the curve have less than 26m of visibility. Mechanical stopping distance at 44km/h exceeds available sightlines.

The Urban Void
